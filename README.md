# Sales-Revenue-Analysis
Performed Operations and visuals for the Kaggle dataset of Sales Data

---
<h1>📊 Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard | Power BI  <br></h1>

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales and revenue data, helping users track business performance and generate actionable insights through visual analytics.<br>

---

<h3>Key Features-</h3><br>
•Imported data from Excel/CSV sources<br>
•Created KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Total Quantity, and Average Order Value<br>
•Visualized revenue trends and top-performing products<br>
•Built interactive charts, filters, and slicers for dynamic analysis<br>
•Generated business insights from sales and profit data<br>
•Data Transformation<br>

---
<h3>Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query:<br></h3>

•Removed duplicate records<br>
•Handled missing values<br>
•Changed data types<br>
•Created Month and Year columns<br>
•Prepared data for reporting and analysis<br>
•DAX Measures Used<br>
•SUM()<br>
•SUMX()<br>
•DISTINCTCOUNT()<br>
•DIVIDE()<br>
•Visualizations<br>
•KPI Cards<br>
•Revenue Trend Line Chart<br>
•Top Products Bar Chart<br>
•Sales by Category Donut Chart<br>
•Profit by Region Chart<br>
•Interactive Slicers and Filters<br>
